TranscribersOfReddit Templates

Do you want to help make images, audio, and video accessible on the internet? These are the templates we developed over six years of operating TranscribersOfReddit, a nonprofit and volunteer community aimed at crowdsourcing accessibility on Reddit.com.

You can bookmark pages you use the most, or start below for a more interactive guide to finding the template that best fits the information you're trying to convey. All of these templates are open to the public; copy and paste them as much as you need.
